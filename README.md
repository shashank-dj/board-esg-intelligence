## Overview

**Board ESG Intelligence** is a governance-focused web application designed for **Private Equity firms, Boards of Directors, and portfolio companies** to systematically **document, validate, and evidence ESG-related decisions** made at board level.

The platform addresses a critical gap in modern ESG practice: while ESG considerations are increasingly discussed in boardrooms, they are rarely captured in a **structured, defensible, and auditable way**.

Board ESG Intelligence provides a **system of record for ESG governance** â€” not ESG reporting.

## The Problem

Regulators, Limited Partners (LPs), and buyers are no longer satisfied with:
- ESG policies
- Sustainability reports
- High-level commitments

They now ask:

This creates governance risk, weak defensibility, and painful LP or audit reviews.

## The Solution

Board ESG Intelligence enables boards and PE firms to:

- Capture ESG-relevant board decisions in a **structured, auditable format**
- Maintain **defensible evidence** of ESG diligence
- Improve governance consistency across portfolio companies
- Respond confidently to LP reviews, regulatory scrutiny, and exit due diligence

---

## Key Features

### ESG Decision Records
- Structured, timestamped board-level ESG decisions
- Decision context, rationale, and trade-offs
- ESG dimensions impacted (Environmental / Social / Governance)
- Supporting evidence and board participation

### Boardroom Assistant (AI-Assisted)
- Reviews decisions for completeness and governance gaps
- Highlights missing ESG considerations
- Improves documentation quality **without replacing human judgment**

> The assistant does **not** make decisions and does **not** train on customer data.

### Personal Board Member View
- Private governance history for individual directors
- Evidence of ESG diligence across portfolio companies

### Exportable Evidence Packs
- Audit-ready outputs for:
  - LP ESG reviews
  - Regulatory inquiries
  - Exit due diligence

The platform is designed with **PE-grade governance expectations**:

- Strict portfolio data isolation  
- Encrypted data storage  
- Immutable audit trails  
- No AI training on customer data
